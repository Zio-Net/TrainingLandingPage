ZioNet Free Training Program for Computer Science Graduates

Kickstart your career in software development with hands-on training, mentorship, and a path to joining our team

Register for Open Day

Our professional training program serves both as a learning experience and an effective screening process, providing equal opportunities to talented candidates across a diverse range of backgrounds and experience.

We evaluate candidates based on skills and potential, while providing participants with the foundational knowledge and best practices needed to prepare them for potential roles within the company.

What You'll Gain

  • Hands-on training in AI Agentic Systems, Full-Stack (React, C#/NodeJS), DevOps, and AI tools – with real teamwork across the full development lifecycle.
  • Mentorship from experienced professionals throughout the process.
  • Opportunity to join our development team.

Who Can Apply

🎓

Academic Background

Recent graduates or final-year Computer Science/related degree students.

*Non-degree candidates with software development experience may also apply.

🌐

English Proficiency

Full proficiency in English – the program is held in English.

🚀

Motivation

Strong commitment to learning and a passion for software development and technology.

Program Structure

0

Open Days & Initial Screening Task

April – May 2026

  • Online open days about the program.
  • Short screening task.
➔
1

Screening Course

During June – July 2026 · 6–9 online sessions

  • Focused course designed to evaluate learning ability and potential.
  • All programming languages are welcome – we focus on conceptual thinking, problem solving, and implementing new approaches and tools.
  • Selected participants will advance to the next stage.
➔
2

Theoretical and Hands-on Training

~4 months · mostly online

  • Theoretical training to strengthen software foundation.
  • Real-world tasks and guided learning.
  • Collaboration with experienced developers and mentors.
➔
3

Employment Opportunity

Program Completion

  • Position offer based on business needs.
  • Integration into ZioNet development teams.
