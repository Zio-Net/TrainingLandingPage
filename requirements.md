# Requirements Document: ZioNet Free Training Program Landing Page

## 1. Project Overview
**Goal:** To recruit high-potential Computer Science graduates for the ZioNet training and screening program.
**Target Audience:** Recent graduates, final-year students, and motivated self-taught developers.
**Primary Conversion Action:** Completing the "Register here" form.
**Timeline:** Program tasks start around April-May 2026; Availability required from June 2026.

## 2. Functional Requirements
*   **Registration Form (displayed inline on the page, not a popup):**
    *   Must collect:
        *   First Name (mandatory, free text)
        *   Last Name (mandatory, free text)
        *   Email Address (mandatory, free text)
        *   Phone Number (mandatory, numeric validation)
        *   Relevant Academic Degree or Relevant Work Experience (mandatory, free text)
        *   LinkedIn Profile (optional, free text)
        *   CV / Resume (optional, file upload)
    *   Input validation for all mandatory fields.
*   **Responsive Design:**
    *   Layout must adapt seamlessly between Desktop and Mobile views (specifically the Process Diagram timeline).
*   **Navigation:**
    *   "Register Now" CTA buttons must anchor/scroll smoothly to the Registration Form section.
*   **External Links:**
    *   Open Day registration link in the Target Audience section.
    *   Company Landing Page link.
    *   About Us page link.
    *   Contact email link.

Junior developer candidates are identified, screened, and trained through a comprehensive hands-on program, preparing them for potential roles within the company."

### Section D: Program Structure (Process Diagram)
*   **Step 0: Open Day & Screening Task (April-May 2026)**
    *   "Join our online open day to learn more about the ZioNet Training Program."
    *   "Interested participants will receive a short screening task to continue the process."
*   **Step 1: Screening Course (6–9 online sessions over one month)**
    *   "A short, focused course designed to evaluate your learning ability and overall potential."
    *   "All programming languages are welcome — we focus on how you think, adapt, and solve problems."
    *   "Only selected participants will advance to the next stage."
*   **Step 2: Theoretical and Hands-on Training (~4 months, mostly online with in-office meetings as needed, flexible)**
    *   "Theoretical training to strengthen your foundation."
    *   "Real-world tasks and guided, structured learning."
    *   "Close collaboration with experienced developers and mentors."
*   **Step 3: Employment Opportunity**
    *   "Candidates who successfully complete the program may be offered a position at ZioNet, in accordance with business needs."
    *   "Integration into ZioNet development teams working on impactful, company-wide projects."
